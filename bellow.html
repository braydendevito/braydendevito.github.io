Multi-Bellow automation fixture

3D Model

For this project, the customer needed an extremely productive weld tooling solution as the volumes for production were very high. The mechanical fixture was designed to operate standalone outside of the welding machines normal motion system. with precise gear placement and the use of bearings, as well as tension based clamping of the part, we were able to produce 20 automated welds in one run before having to stop and reload the fixture. This tooling exceeded the customers expectations, and production runs were much more efficient post-weld tooling manufacture.
